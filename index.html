About Me

Allow me to tell you a bit about myself, my name is Roberto and as you might have noticed, I'm a Data Analyst. I was brought up in Chihuahua, Mexico, and I discovered the beauty of Data some years ago.

My first interaction working with Data was at a local pharmacy's warehouse, I noticed that nobody knew the exact number of medications and medical tools that were in stock, So I told the owner we could start logging the entries and uploading them into the system. That's when my Data journey began, and it hasn't stoped since.

I love looking at Data and making sense of it, since it can provide us with many insights. I also love traveling and taking pictures of my surroundings, just like the one shown above.

Thank you for stopping by my portfolio website! I hope you found all the information you were looking for in a clear and concise way.
